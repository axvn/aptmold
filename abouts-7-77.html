Our service includes as below:
Product analysis & suggestion
Product design 
Moldflow analysisMould design

Whole project analysis & summarization

Our engineering team will be glad to provide you with valuable advice for free during the whole process!
 
We use software for design as below:
2D: Drawing:  Auto-CAD, E-drawing
3D: Modeling: Pro/ENGINEER , SolidWorks, Unigraphics, Moldflow

We also use international file formats as below:
2D:  dwg ,  dxf , pdf , jpg, tif
3D:  step , Igs , X-T , prt , sldprt

1. How many molds could you make every month?
About 80 SETS.

2.How about the largest mold you ever made?
2000mm*1600mm*1000mm.

3.How about the tolerance?
Mold tolerance 0.005 mm, Product tolerance 0.02 mm

4.Could you make double color injection mold?
Yes.

5.Have you ever made over mold ?
What kind of materials you use? Yes. We have great experience in over mold of ABS+PC, PA+TPE, ABS+TPE, TPE+TPE. 

6.How about the steel you usually use?
We use steel of P20, P20H, 718H, NAK80, 1.2311, H13, 1.2344, 420SS, S136, S-7 and  1.2767. They are all from famous suppliers, such as LKM, ASSAB, THYSSEN and DAIDO.

7.How about the standard components you use?
We only use standard components from LKM, DAIDO, HASCO, DME.


8.How long do you need for quotation?
Exact quotation will be available within 1~2 working days once we get sufficient information from customer.


9.What do you need for quotation?
We need 2D product drawing & 3D file, or specific sample with detailed explanation, and mold specification.


10.What is your turnaround time for injection mold building?
Usually 3-5 weeks, it also depends on mold size and structure complexity. We can work overnight for you urgent project to shorten the delivery.
